My Creative Process

Below is the process I follow when creating digital design projects.

Editing in Photoshop

Step-by-Step Process

It's important to choose what you want to convey, then find suitable images and a color palette.

  1. Find at least 5 images that you like.
  2. The background color is important because it will determine what you want to convey.
  3. You should check if it looks good with "normal, overlay, subtract, etc" so that each image blends together.
  4. Set the text color to the same color as the background and play around with the position and deformation of the text.
  5. Then you find a skateboard mockup or a template.
